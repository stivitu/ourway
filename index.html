Challenges and solutions

Today's Internet is a long way from what it was in originally intended - and that is a FREE exchange of information. This days, in order to find relevant information, you often have to search a bunch of useless content, some of which are also dangerous for our computers. And everything is sold. From information to programs that you will often use that once and never again. I am also a user, and I know which bothers me. First of all, in order to get some information, I have to accept a bunch of "cookies", which later work in the background and slow down my computer. Sites use all possible tricks to get our e-mail addresses and personal data. All the time they monitor our habits and activity on the Internet. We are sent advertising messages and e-mails, for the suspension of which you sometimes need to spend an entire afternoon. None of us wants or needs that. We need an internet with easily accessible information and easy to use. Everyone wants to have their own framework, everyone says that their system is the best. Which results in a lot of confusing material. Does our landing page really have to be 200-300 mb!? I use on this page only my own minified photos. The whole page will not have more then 2 mb content. About useless content, in the form of completely useless videos, barren self-promotion and sites that offer nothing, to say the least. These "contents" only slow down the flow of information, take up space on servers, whose fans do nothing but heat the atmosphere. And the responsibility is on all of us. And that's why, as always, we have to start with ourselves. Use technology which is made available to us reasonably and with measure - to begin with. I also see the solution in "light-weight" applications, without unused installed packages, photos and other materials. Big companies they only care about profit. We believe that we should also think about the happiness of the individual, care for society and the community as well as environmental protection. In order for things to move in a more positive direction, we will not and cannot change the world. But we can change ourselves, and act in the segment we have influence over. In order for our society to finally become a happier society, and ultimately we would also have a faster, better and greener internet!
